Our Story

The Nashon Okowa Mentorship Program (NOMP) is a free mentorship program for students and recent graduates in the Built Environment industry. The aim of the program is to equip the mentees with not only the professional knowledge of the built environment but even more materially is to provided them with the much needed, yet lacking, personal skills to get their careers started.

NOMP aim is to develop unique and bespoke mentees that blossom into leaders of the industry and country through their incredible impact to the profession and society.
