Meet the Mentor


Nashon Okowa holds a Bachelor’s degree in Construction Management (Hons) the University of Nairobi. He has built an astounding career as a Construction Project Manager with extensive experience in managing projects ranging from; Residential, Commercials, Malls, Hotels, and Industrial plants in East Africa.
Currently he is the Managing Director of Beacon Africa Consultants – a project and construction management consultancy firm based in Nairobi.

Mr. Okowa is the immediate past Chairman of the Association of Construction Managers of Kenya (ACMK) and also an author of the books: ‘The Abnormal Student’, ‘Don’t Buy That House’, & ‘The 7 Rules of Real Estate Investment.’ He has greatly contributed to the advancement of the project management profession in the region through various forums and activities.

Nashon is an advisory council member of the South Africa Construction Management Foundation (CMF). In addition, he is a regular columnist for Standard Newspaper & The People Daily and has written extensively on issues pertaining to the construction and real estate sector in Kenya
